Welcome to ADIT!

ADIT (Automatic DICOM Transfer) allows to transfer DICOM data between different servers or download the files (optionally into an encrypted archive) to a network folder.

"Batch transfer" can be used to transfer or download multiple studies of different patients using an Excel sheet with defined studies.
